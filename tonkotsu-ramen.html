Japanese Tonkotsu Ramen Recipe

Japanese Tonkotsu Ramen

Description

Very hard to make, but it's worth the price.

Meta-Data

Prep Time: 30 minutes
Cook Time: 12 hours
Servings: 4 bowls
Calories: 1262 calories per bowl?

Ingredients

Tonkotsu Ramen

  • Tonkotsu Pork Broth (8 cups)
  • Good Quality Dried Ramen Noodles (12 ounces)
  • Large Or Extra Large Eggs (4 eggs)
  • Enoki Or Other Mushrooms (3 ounces)
  • Thinly Sliced Green Onions (2 stalks)

Chashu Pork Belly

  • Pork Belly (2 lbs)
  • Soy Sauce (1/4 cup)
  • Sake (1/2 cup)
  • Mirin (1/2 cup)
  • Sugar (1/4 cup)
  • Garlic (3 cloves)
  • Coarsely Chopped Green Onions (2 stalks)

Soy Bacon Tare

  • Good Quality Bacon (2 slices)
  • Soy Sauce (1/4 cup)
  • Mirin (2 tbps)
  • Sake (2 tbps)
  • Shiro Miso (4 tbps)

Miso Tare

  • Shiro Miso (1/2 cup)
  • Sake (1/4 cup)
  • Mirin (1/4 cup)
  • Kosher Salt (3/2 tsp)
  • Shichimi Togarashi (1 pinch, optional)
