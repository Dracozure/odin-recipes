Korean Pork Bowl Recipe

Korean Pork Bowl

Description

Korean pork bowls are simply delicious. Consisting of Gochujang pork over rice and veggies, it is cheap and easy to make.
It is also very nutrious and can last a while in the fridge, which makes it an excellent dish for someone who is very busy and cannot cook on a daily basis.

Ingredients

General

  • Samyang Buldak Ramen (1 pack)
  • Green Onions (2 stalks)
  • Garlic (3 cloves)
  • Pork Belly (600 g)
    Note: Chicken or beef can be substituted
  • Neutral-tasting oil (1/2 tbps)
  • Soft-boiled/Fried Eggs (1-2 eggs, optional)
  • Toasted sesame seeds garnish (1 pinch, optional)

Gojuchang Samgyupsal Sauce

  • Gochujang (2 tbps)
  • Gochugaru (1 tbps)
  • Sugar (1 tbps)
  • Oyster Sauce (1/2 tbps)
  • Soy Sauce (1 tbps)
  • Corn Syrup (1 tbps)
  • Mirin (2 tbps)
  • Black Pepper (2 pinches)
  • Cornstarch (1 tsp)
  • MSG (1 pinch, optional)

Steps

Prep Ingredients

  1. Thinly slice all the green onions, optionally seperating a little bit of the green leafy part for garnish
  2. Cut meat into bite-sized pieces
  3. In a bowl, mix together all the ingredients for the Gojuchang Samgyupsal Sauce
